Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through technology, startups, and the "Digital Bangladesh" initiative.

Sustainability

Promoting resilience with renewable energy, green technology, and climate adaptation efforts.

Infrastructure

Transforming connectivity via bridges, metro systems, and economic development projects.

Empowerment

Uplifting communities through education, microfinance, and inclusive skill development programs.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"As we embark on the journey of building a new Bangladesh, let us embrace innovation, resilience, and unity. Our vision is one of prosperity and equality, where every citizen contributes to and benefits from our nation’s growth. Together, we can overcome challenges, uphold integrity, and create a legacy of hope and opportunity for future generations. Let us unite to shape a brighter, sustainable future for Bangladesh."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
